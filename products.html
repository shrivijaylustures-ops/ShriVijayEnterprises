Ceramic Colours


Our Ceramic Colours are formulated for precision, durability, and brilliant surface finish. We offer specialized colours for both on-glaze and in-glaze applications:

● Glass Colours (500–600°C): Designed for smooth, consistent application on glassware with excellent firing stability.

● On-Glaze Colours (3rd Firing, 760–830°C): Provide sharp shades and high gloss, ideal for overglaze decoration on ceramic products.

● In-Glaze Colours (1050–1150°C): Engineered to fuse perfectly into the glaze layer, delivering long-lasting colour depth, scratch resistance, and thermal stability.

Our imported Ceramic Colours are trusted by manufacturers for their purity, firing consistency, and excellent reproduction of shades.

Liquid Lustre Colours


Our Lustre Colours bring rich, metallic brilliance to ceramic and glass surfaces. Every batch is crafted for smooth application, uniform shine, and perfect firing results.

● For Glassware (500–600°C): Designed to achieve a bright, reflective finish with clean firing behaviour on all types of glass items.

● For Ceramicware (3rd Firing, 760–830°C): Creates a shimmering metallic sheen, ideal for decorative ceramics, giftware, and premium tableware.

With decades of expertise, our lustres are known for stable viscosity, high coverage, and a consistent finish across firing cycles.

Our Liquid Gold and Platinum Lustre ranges are formulated to deliver a luxurious metallic finish with exceptional adhesion and firing reliability.

● Available in 7%, 10%, 12%, and Custom Purity: Suitable for both glass (500–600°C) and ceramic third firing (760–830°C), offering unmatched, brilliance and smooth, premium coverage.

● Each product is refined to provide:

✔ Rich metallic shine

✔ High durability after firing

✔ Smooth, streak-free application

✔ Uniform colour even at lower firing temperatures
