About Us

Our Journey

The legacy of "श्री" Vijay Enterprises began in 1975 with the establishment of Spectrum India, a pioneer in the manufacturing of Lustre Colours and specialty decorative materials for the ceramic industry.

In 2001, the business evolved into Vijay Enterprises, expanding its product range, refining formulations, and becoming a trusted supplier to ceramic and glass manufacturers across India.

Continuing this legacy of excellence, 2025 marks a new chapter with the formation of "श्री" Vijay Enterprises—a renewed identity that carries forward five decades of expertise, innovation, and customer trust.

Who We Are Today

At "श्री" Vijay Enterprises, we specialize in:

  • ✓ Manufacturing Lustre Colours for ceramicware and glassware

  • ✓ Producing Liquid Gold & Platinum Lustre in multiple purity grades

  • ✓ Importing premium Ceramic Colours for third-firing decoration

  • ✓ Supplying high-performance On-Glaze, In-Glaze, and Glass Colours

Across every product line, our focus remains consistent—precision, purity, consistent firing results, and superior visual finish.
