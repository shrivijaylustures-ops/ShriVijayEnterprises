Trusted Makers Of Liquid Lustre, Liquid Gold & Ceramic Colours


At “श्री” Vijay Enterprises, we specialize in manufacturing high-quality Lustre Colours, Liquid Gold, and Platinum Lustre, and are trusted importers of premium Ceramic Colours for third-firing decoration across ceramic and glass industries.


With a heritage that began in 1975 as Spectrum India, evolved into Vijay Enterprises (2001–2025), and now continues as “श्री” Vijay Enterprises, our commitment to innovation, purity, and craftsmanship remains unchanged.


Backed by over five decades of technical excellence, rooted in the vision of Late Mr. Umesh Agarwal, a Metallurgical Engineer from IIT Bombay, our legacy continues under the leadership of Mr. Hemant Agarwal. Today, we proudly serve leading manufacturers with advanced colour solutions that offer exceptional consistency, long-lasting durability, and unmatched brilliance.
