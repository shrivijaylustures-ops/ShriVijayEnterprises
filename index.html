About Us

Our story

We are the manufacturers of Lustre colours and Liquid Gold and importers of Ceramic Colours for third-firing decorations for ceramic and glass ware. Since 1975, we are manufacturing Lustre colours and formed a firm in 2002 named VIJAY ENTERPRISES. In 2025 we formed a new firm named “श्री” VIJAY ENTERPRISES and are continuing the same business with the new firm name.

What we make

  • Lustre Colours for high and low temperature firings
  • Liquid Gold Luster in multiple concentrations
  • Ceramic Colours — domestic & imported

Why choose us

Decades of experience, strict quality control, and a focus on reliable supply for ceramic decorators and glass artists. We provide product customization and technical support for application and firing.

Core strengths
  • Premium raw materials
  • Technical guidance
  • Custom formulations
